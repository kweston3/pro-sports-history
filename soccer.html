Basketball was invented by Dr. James Naismith in 1891 as a way to keep his students active during the winter months. The sport quickly grew in popularity and became a staple in American culture, eventually spreading worldwide.

The game has evolved significantly over the years, with changes to the rules, playing styles, and the overall pace of the game. Today, basketball is played at various levels across the globe, from local community games to professional leagues.
